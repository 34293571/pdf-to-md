# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 15, 2026

PDF a MD is a personal project operated by an individual developer ("I", "me", or "my"). I run **pdfamd.com**, a free online tool that converts PDF files to Markdown. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle information when you use the website.

## 1. Summary

- I do **not** require an account to use PDF a MD.
- PDF files you upload are processed **in memory** for conversion and are **not stored** on my servers after the request completes.
- I do **not** sell your personal information.
- PDF a MD is **not** operated by a registered company.

## 2. Information I Process

### 2.1 PDF files you upload

When you convert a file, your PDF is transmitted to my conversion service so I can return Markdown output. The file is used only to perform that conversion.

### 2.2 Technical data

Like most websites, hosting and infrastructure providers may automatically process limited technical information, such as:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- Date and time of request
- Pages visited and general usage events (e.g., conversion started, error type)
- Referring URL

I use this information only to operate, secure, and improve the service.

### 2.3 Information I do not collect

I do not ask for your name, email address, or payment information to use the free converter.

## 3. How I Use Information

I use information to:

- Provide the PDF to Markdown conversion service
- Enforce usage limits (file size, page count, and file type checks)
- Monitor performance, errors, and abuse
- Maintain security and reliability
- Improve PDF a MD and pdfamd.com

I do **not** use uploaded PDF content for advertising profiles, and I do **not** sell uploaded content to third parties.

## 4. File Storage and Retention

- Uploaded PDFs are processed in **memory**.
- I do **not** intentionally save your PDF or the generated Markdown to persistent storage after the conversion request ends.
- Temporary data in memory exists only for the duration needed to complete the request.

Network and hosting providers may process traffic in the ordinary course of delivering the service. I do not use that processing to build a library of user documents.

## 5. Third-Party Services

I use third-party providers to host and operate the website and conversion API, including:

- **Vercel** — website hosting and delivery
- **Render** — PDF conversion API

These providers process data on my behalf as needed to run the service. Their own privacy policies govern their practices.

If I enable analytics or error monitoring in the future, I will update this Privacy Policy to name those tools and describe what they collect.

## 6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

PDF a MD may use:

- **Essential technologies** required for basic site functionality, if any
- **Analytics technologies** (only if I enable analytics) to understand how visitors use pdfamd.com

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect certain features.

## 7. Data Security

I use reasonable measures appropriate for a small, free online tool, including HTTPS encryption in transit. No method of transmission or processing is completely secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Sensitive Content

PDF a MD is a free online utility. **Do not upload** files containing highly sensitive, confidential, medical, legal, or financial information unless you accept the risks of using a personal, internet-based conversion tool.

## 9. Children's Privacy

PDF a MD is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. International Users

pdfamd.com may be accessed from different countries. If you use the service from outside the region where my hosting providers operate, your information may be processed in those locations.

## 11. Your Choices

- **Do not upload** content you do not want processed for conversion.
- **Use at your discretion** — if you prefer not to upload a file, do not use the converter for that document.
- **Browser controls** — manage cookies and local storage through your browser.

Because I do not operate user accounts and do not intentionally retain uploaded files, I generally cannot retrieve or delete a specific past upload after a request has finished.

## 12. Data Protection Requests

Because I do not operate user accounts and do not intentionally retain uploaded PDF files, I generally do not hold personal data that can be linked to you beyond short-lived technical logs handled by hosting providers.

If you have a privacy-related question or request, email me at **shua6715@gmail.com**. I will respond within a reasonable time.
